**Prototype:** GemRB.GameSelectPCSingle (PartyID)

**Description:** Selects one PC in non-walk environment (i.e. in shops, 
inventory, ...).

**Parameters:**
  * PartyID - the PC's position in the party

**Return value:** N/A
